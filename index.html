About Me

Hello! I'm Christina (Styna) Lo and I graduated from the University of Calgary with a Bachelor of Science in Geomatics Engineering and a Minor in Computer Science. I have a variety of "hobbies" and I love all things science, tech, music, fashion, design, art, diy, etc etc.

My current projects include creating my own website (check), writing and producing my own music, redecorating my living space, and most importantly, starting my career as an engineer. This site is a method for me to combine all of my passions and document my process as each project progresses. Hopefully there is a method to this madness and maybe I can help someone with my experiences along the way. :)
